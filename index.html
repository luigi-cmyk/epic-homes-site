Common Questions

Everything you're
wondering about.

The honest answers to what most people ask before they pick up the phone.

How much does it cost to build a custom home in MA, NH, RI or CT? +

For 2026, custom homes in our region typically run $350–$600+ per square foot, depending on lot conditions, finishes and complexity. A 2,500 sq ft modern farmhouse usually lands between $875K and $1.5M turnkey. We give you a real ballpark in your first consultation — no fake "starting at" numbers.

How long does it take from start to finish? +

From signed contract to move-in day, plan on 10–14 months for most custom builds. Design takes 6–10 weeks, permitting 4–8 weeks (varies by town), and construction 7–10 months. We'll give you a realistic schedule before you commit, and we hit it.

Do I need to own land before contacting you? +

Not at all. We help clients find and evaluate land regularly — we know which lots are buildable, what the zoning costs really look like, and where the hidden site-prep expenses hide. If you already have a lot, we'll do a free buildability assessment before you spend a dollar on design.

Can you work with my architect, or do you have one? +

Both work. We have in-house and partnered architects we've built dozens of homes with — they know our crews, costs, and pace. But if you've already hired an architect you love, we'll happily collaborate. We're builders, not gatekeepers.

How does construction financing work? +

Most clients use a construction-to-permanent loan from a local lender (we can introduce you to a few we trust). The bank funds the build in stages as work is completed, then converts to a regular mortgage at move-in. We handle all the lender draw paperwork — you don't need to chase invoices.

What's included in the price — and what's not? +

Our prices include everything: design, permits, materials, labor, site work, finishes, appliances, and final cleanup — the home is delivered turnkey. The only things outside are your furniture and any custom landscaping beyond the basics. The price you see is the price you pay. Period.

What kind of warranty do you provide? +

2-year workmanship warranty on everything we build, plus 10-year structural warranty per Massachusetts builder standards. But more importantly, we answer the phone after move-in. Most builders disappear at handover — we don't.

Do you build in my town? +

We build across Massachusetts, New Hampshire, Rhode Island and Connecticut — including Andover, Westford, Lowell, Brockton, Hudson, Nashua, Bedford, Concord, Manchester, Providence, Hartford, and dozens more. If you're in the four-state area, we can build there. Just ask.
